About Us

We are a Cyprus-based deep-tech start-up specialising in advanced RF hardware and metamaterials for mission critical defence and security systems. Our work includes miniature wideband antennas, low-observable metasurfaces, polarization-shaping structures, and Reconfigurable Intelligent Surfaces, supported by high-fidelity simulation, prototyping, and expert RF consulting.
